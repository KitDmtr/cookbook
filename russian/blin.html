Blini

Light and fluffy pancakes perfect for appetizers or breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm milk (105°F to 115°F)
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the buckwheat flour, all-purpose flour, yeast, and sugar. Make a well in the center.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm milk and egg yolk. Pour the wet ingredients into the well of the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the batter rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  3. Stir in the salt and melted butter. Heat a lightly greased non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. For each blini, pour about 2 tablespoons of batter onto the pan.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip the blini and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. Serve blini warm with your favorite toppings, such as smoked salmon, crème fraîche, caviar, chopped chives, or jam.
