Borsch

Classic Russian soup

Ingredients

  • 3 medium beets, peeled and grated
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 8 cups chicken broth, plus 2 cups water
  • 3 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • (Optional) 1 pound beef chuck or stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill, for garnish
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the grated beets and saut√© for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.
  2. (Optional) If using meat, brown the beef in the same pot with another tablespoon of olive oil. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
  3. Add the chicken broth, water, potatoes, carrots, and onion to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are almost tender.
  4. Stir in the garlic, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine vinegar, sugar, and bay leaf. Bring back to a simmer and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. (Optional) Add the browned beef back to the pot and heat through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  7. Ladle the borscht into bowls and garnish with fresh dill and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy!
